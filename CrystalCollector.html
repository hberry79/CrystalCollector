CrystalsCollector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game under "number to match."

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.

Be careful, if you go over the number to match, you lose that round!
